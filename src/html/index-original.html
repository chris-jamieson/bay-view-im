Site overview

Situated in Port St. Mary on the corner of Primrose Terrace and Lhargan Road, the Bay View is 3 storeys high with a basement area. The existing building and outriggers cover the majority of the site, leaving a small internal courtyard.

In addition to the main footprint, a parcel of land located across the road at the rear of the building and a small garden (optional purchase) located on the opposite side of Primrose Terrace.

The neighbouring properties are predominantly residential, with some commercial properties nearby.

The Bay View Hotel ceased operating commercially as a public house and hotel a number of years prior to being purchased by the current owner in 2015.
