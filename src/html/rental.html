Site overview

Situated in Port St. Mary on the corner of Bay View Road and Lhargan Road, the Bay View Hotel is 3 storeys high with a basement area. The existing building and outriggers cover the majority of the site, leaving a small internal courtyard.

In addition to the main footprint, a parcel of land located across the road at the rear of the building and a small garden located on the opposite side of Bay View Road.

The neighbouring properties are predominantly residential, with some commercial properties nearby.

The Bay View Hotel ceased operating commercially as a public house and hotel a number of years prior to being purchased by the current owner in 2015.
